OPTIONS ANALYTICS — FORMULA APPENDIX (UNITS & DEFINITIONS)

Notation & Units
• Rates are decimals per year unless noted.
• Basis flags: annual vs continuous.
• Time: T in years; trading days per year ≈ 252; months per year = 12.
• Dividends: continuous yield q (per year).

Compounding (conversions)
• r_{\text{cont}} = \ln(1 + r_{\text{annual}}) 
• r_{\text{annual}} = e^{r_{\text{cont}}} - 1

Forward & Discounting
• F = S_0 \, e^{(r - q)T}
• \text{DF}(r,T) = e^{-rT}

Geometric Brownian Motion (price dynamics)
• \frac{dS_t}{S_t} = \mu \, dt + \sigma \, dW_t
• S_T = S_0 \exp\!\Big((\mu - \tfrac{1}{2}\sigma^2)T + \sigma\sqrt{T}\,Z\Big),\quad Z \sim \mathcal{N}(0,1)

Risk-Neutral vs Real-World Drift
• \mu_{\text{RN}} = r - q
• \mu_{\text{RW}} = r_f + \beta \cdot \mathrm{ERP} - q
• \mathrm{ERP} = E(R_m) - r_f

CAPM Drift (service /api/capm)
• \mu = r_f + \beta \cdot (E(R_m) - r_f) - q = r_f + \beta \cdot \mathrm{ERP} - q

Annualization (generic interval \Delta)
• \mu_{\text{geom}} = \bar{r}_{\Delta} \cdot p_y
• \mu_{\text{arith}} = \overline{R_{\Delta}} \cdot p_y
• \sigma = s_{r_{\Delta}} \cdot \sqrt{p_y}
Where:
  r_{\Delta} = \ln\!\left(\frac{P_t}{P_{t-1}}\right),\quad R_{\Delta} = \frac{P_t}{P_{t-1}} - 1
  p_y = \begin{cases} 252 & (\Delta = 1\text{d}) \\ 12 & (\Delta = 1\text{mo}) \end{cases}

Beta & Diagnostics (OLS with intercept)
• \beta = \dfrac{\operatorname{Cov}(R_s, R_m)}{\operatorname{Var}(R_m)}
• \alpha = \bar{R}_s - \beta \bar{R}_m
• R^2 = 1 - \dfrac{\sum (R_s - (\alpha + \beta R_m))^2}{\sum (R_s - \bar{R}_s)^2}
• \mathrm{SE}(\beta) = \sqrt{ \dfrac{\mathrm{SSE}/(n-2)}{\sum (R_m - \bar{R}_m)^2} }

Black–Scholes Components (risk-neutral)
• d_1 = \dfrac{\ln(S_0/K) + (r - q + \tfrac{1}{2}\sigma^2)T}{\sigma\sqrt{T}}
• d_2 = d_1 - \sigma\sqrt{T}
• \mathbb{E}[(S_T - K)^+] = S_0 e^{-qT} N(d_1) - K e^{-rT} N(d_2)
• \mathbb{E}[(K - S_T)^+] = K e^{-rT} N(-d_2) - S_0 e^{-qT} N(-d_1)

Probability of Profit (PoP) under lognormal S_T
• z_K = \dfrac{\ln(K/S_0) - (\mu - \tfrac{1}{2}\sigma^2)T}{\sigma\sqrt{T}}
• \mathbb{P}(S_T > B) = 1 - \Phi(z_B) \quad ; \quad \mathbb{P}(S_T < B) = \Phi(z_B)
• Interval L < S_T < H: \mathrm{PoP} = \Phi(z_H) - \Phi(z_L)
• Union of disjoint intervals \bigcup_i [L_i,H_i]: \mathrm{PoP} = \sum_i\big(\Phi(z_{H_i}) - \Phi(z_{L_i})\big)

Expected Profit / Expected Loss (P&L X)
• \mathrm{EP} = \mathbb{E}[X^{+}], \quad \mathrm{EL} = \mathbb{E}[X^{-}]
• \mathbb{E}[X] = \mathrm{EP} - \mathrm{EL}, \quad \mathrm{PoP} = \mathbb{P}(X > 0)

P&L Convention (premium and financing)
• X = \text{Payoff}_T - \Pi_0 \, e^{rT}
• Sign: premiums paid today are positive cash outflows; report EP/EL as positive magnitudes.

Edge Cases (sanity)
• \text{Arbitrage box} \Rightarrow \mathrm{PoP} \approx 1 \quad ; \quad \text{Certain loss} \Rightarrow \mathrm{PoP} = 0
• Guard basis consistency: when mixing inputs, convert via r_{\text{cont}} \leftrightarrow r_{\text{annual}}.
